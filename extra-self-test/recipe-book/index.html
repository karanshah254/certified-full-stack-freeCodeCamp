Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

This creamy chocolate fudge is a rich and indulgent treat that's perfect for chocolate lovers.

Easy to make and perfect for sharing, this fudge recipe will satisfy any sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, melt chocolate chips, sweetened condensed milk, and butter over low heat.
  2. Remove from heat, stir in vanilla extract and salt, then pour mixture into a lined pan.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours until firm.
  4. Cut into squares and serve.
